Our Testing Process

A comprehensive, structured approach to ensure your software meets the highest quality standards.

1

Free Discovery & Requirements Review

The project's designated Test Manager leads an in-depth review of your project requirements, user flows and technical specifications. This ensures we fully understand the scope, critical functionality, business objectives and determine the scope and depth of testing needed. This stage ensures full alignment before any work begins, and provides the basis for the estimate.

2

Estimation & Project Outline

Following the requirement review, we provide a high level estimate covering expected timelines, resource needs, and cost. This includes an overview of testing phases, environments, and potential risks. You'll receive a transparent overview of what's involved, allowing you to plan confidently before moving forward.

3

Test Strategy & Planning

Based on the requirements, we craft a comprehensive test strategy and a detailed test plan, ensuring full coverage across functional, quality performance, and edge case scenarios. This includes defining test objectives, methodologies, environments, entry/exit criteria, and reporting structures. Our planning ensures testing is structured, measurable, and aligned with your development lifecycle. During this process, the project's designated Test Manager will detect potential edge cases that may not have been planned for during development, giving the plus of tightening development before the test execution even starts.

4

Environment & Test Data Setup

We prepare all required test environments, accounts, configurations, and datasets to mirror real-world scenarios. Proper setup ensures accurate, reliable testing results and reduces delays caused by environment readiness issues.

5

Test Execution — Manual, Automated, or Hybrid

Our skilled team conducts thorough testing across functionality, usability, performance, and integration layers. We adapt our approach to your needs—running manual tests, automation scripts, or a blended model. Each test run is tracked for full transparency.

6

Transparent Reporting & Defect Documentation

You receive clear, concise reporting throughout the project, including real-time defect tracking and progress updates. All defects are documented with clear steps to reproduce, evidence, severity levels, and recommendations. We also schedule regular sync meetings to keep you informed every step of the way.

7

Re-Testing, Regression & Final Quality Verification

Once fixes are deployed, we re-test, run regression cycles, and validate all affected areas. Final verification ensures the product meets requirements, functions reliably, and is ready for release with confidence.

8

Quality Report & Recommendations

At project completion, we deliver a comprehensive final report summarising the full scope of testing performed, all defects identified, outstanding risks (if any), and expert recommendations for ongoing quality improvement.

Expert-Led Testing From Start to Finish

Every project is overseen by highly experienced, fully qualified Senior Test Managers. They lead the requirements review, estimation, and full test planning—ensuring your project begins with a strong, strategic foundation. Because we believe: a project well planned is a project well delivered.
