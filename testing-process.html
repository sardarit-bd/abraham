Our Testing Process

A comprehensive, structured approach to ensure your software meets the highest quality standards

  1. 1

    Free Discovery & Requirements Review

    The project's designated Test Manager leads an in-depth review of your project requirements, user flows, and technical specifications. This ensures full understanding of the scope, critical functionality, and business objectives.

  2. 2

    Estimation & Project Outline

    We provide a high-level estimate covering timelines, resource needs, and cost. You'll get an overview of testing phases, environments, and potential risks for confident planning.

  3. 3

    Test Strategy & Planning

    We craft a comprehensive test strategy and detailed test plan, covering functional, performance, and edge cases. Entry/exit criteria and reporting structures ensure structured, measurable testing.

  4. 4

    Environment & Test Data Setup

    All required test environments, accounts, and datasets are prepared to mirror real-world scenarios, ensuring accurate results and reduced delays.
